Reconnect with nature

Why visit?

Cirencester park, spread out across more than 1000 hectares, boasts beautiful, unspoiled woodland, open to the public every day of the year.
With hundreds of miles of dog-friendly paths to choose from, discover the natural splendour of the park today!

About

Facilities

Cirencester Park has plenty of facilities to offer visitors. By the main entrance of the park, you will find toilets, including a baby-changing area.

There is also a food truck, offering hot and cold drinks, as well as a variety of snacks. A seating area is provided.

Horse riders and responsible dog walkers are welcome in the park; We kindly ask the latter to to clean up after their dogs, and to keep them on leashes when travelling through fields with livestock.
